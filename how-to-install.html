HOW TO INSTALL

Ready to Go? Utilize Rinku Best Practices when Installing.

Rinku is not a registration platform, but rather a social-supercharging plugin to amplify your event across the social media landscape of your attendees. Ensure best practices by following the instructions outlined below when installing InGo.

GUIDED INSTALLATION AND TESTING

Click below to install and conduct quality assurance for your event!

Install & QA My Event

INFOGRAPHIC FOR INSTALLATION

  • 1. STARTING REGISTRATION - LOGIN (ACTIVATOR) WIDGET

    The Rinku Login (Activator) Widget initiates the social-amplification. Registrants start by connecting socially and authorizing the Rinku app.

    • Embed the Login Widget using the script below, inserting your specific widget ID.
    • Ensure that the Login Widget is prominently displayed and the only social media call to action.
    • Only use our modal pop-up option when you cannot embed. Notify your Rinku Account Manager to activate this feature

  • 2. AUTOFILLING REGISTRATION DATA - REGISTRATION (AUTOFILLER) WIDGET

    Rinku can help speed up registration by autofilling social media profile data to your registration forms.

    • Embed Autofill whenever you need to populate attendee data that InGo can map. For example, if you have multiple locations that you need autofill to occur, be sure to place the Autofill snippet wherever it’s needed. Only 1 Autofill snippet per page is necessary.

  • 3. DISPLAYING THE SOCIAL (AMPLIFIER) WIDGET

    Rinku can help your attendees search, discover and invite people in their networks through the use of our Social snippet. Ensure best practices by adhering to the following:

    • Embed the Social Widget on your confirmation page.
    • Ensure the Social Widget is prominently displayed and the only social media call-to-action

  • 4. CONFIGURING THE CONFIRMATION (AUTHORIZER) WIDGET

    Through the use of Rinku’s Confirmation Widget snippet, Rinku posts on the user’s timeline/status or feed, depending on which option he/she selecting at Login. Our Confirmation snippet also ensures accurate data is being sent from the registration flow to ensure duplicates are not created.

    • Embed the Confirmation Widget at the end of the process after purchase/ticket/registration completion.
    • In a typical registration flow, you embed both the Confirmation Widget and the Socail widget on your platform’s confirmation page.
    • Embed the Confirmation Widget before embedding the Social Widget snippet. This ensures that our confirmation beacon fires first and reports accurate data and posts right away.
    • IMPORTANT: In some cases, you must configure the Confirmation Widget to send us the actual data values on the final page.
